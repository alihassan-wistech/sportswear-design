— OUR STORY

Built on Craftsmanship

Founded in 2009 in Sialkot, Pakistan—the historic heart of the world's sporting goods manufacturing industry—Deesha Industries began with a single mission: to provide uncompromising quality to global athletic brands.

Over the years, we have scaled our operations from a local stitching unit to a full-fledged OEM and Private Label manufacturing powerhouse. Today, we supply custom sportswear, activewear, and leisurewear to brands, teams, and wholesalers across 50+ countries.

We combine traditional tailoring expertise with modern performance fabrics and cutting-edge sublimation technology. Our team of skilled artisans and engineers work meticulously to ensure every stitch, print, and cut meets rigorous international standards.

— OUR MISSION

Empowering Global Brands

To empower sportswear brands of all sizes by providing them with seamless, high-quality, and reliable manufacturing solutions. We aim to be the invisible force behind your brand's success on the field and in the gym.

— OUR VISION

Sustainable Innovation
